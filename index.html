The Discovery Model

Learning Through Discovery

At Inspire, we believe each child brings a unique personality and learning style. Our philosophy is rooted in Lev Vygotsky's "discovery model of learning" — children's learning develops naturally through various paths of discovery.

Vygotsky saw learning as a social and collaborative activity. Children "construct" knowledge based on what they already know. Our teachers act as facilitators — asking the right questions at the right moment to unlock each child's potential.

Core Beliefs

We Believe Children…

1

Are self-motivated, curious learners at heart

2

Learn powerfully by watching the people around them

3

Learn through meaningful repetition and practice

4

Learn how to treat others by the way they are treated

5

Learn best when surrounded by people who genuinely care about them
